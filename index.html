ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Aaron, a 17-year-old aspiring game developer from Germany.
I've been developing games since I was 7 years old — sort of. Through game development, I've come into contact with many areas of art: programming, 3D modeling, texturing, level design, game design, sound design, and music composition.
I've been exploring all these areas as much as my time allows, and due to my curiosity about all these topics, I've become somewhat of a generalist. This means I know enough about most things to do them, but not enough to do them professionally.
Still, I haven't been sitting around! So, please enjoy a few select projects that I've been a part of—or made myself—below.
Thank you for visiting my little corner of the web, have fun!

Music / Soundtrack

NEW RELEASE EVERY FRIDAY

Color Convert

Misc / Tools

Color Convert came to be out of the need to constantly convert RGB colours in a 0-255 scale to a 0-1 float scale for IMGUI. That's why I decided to write this quick web-gui to make exactly that conversion easier. Sure... I could have built the functionality in code, but this seemed like the better option at the time.
Oh well...
