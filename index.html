ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Aaron - A 17 Year old aspiring Game-Developer from Germany.
I have been developing games since I was 7 years old - well, I've been trying to at least.
Through game-development I've come into contact with many areas of art: Programming, 3d-Modelling, Texturing, Level-Design, Game-Design, Sound-Design, and Music-Composition.
I've been exploring all these areas as much as my time allows and due to my curiosity about all of these topics I've become somewhat of a generalist (which is just a fancy way of saying that I know enough about most things to do them, but not enough about them to do them professionally). Still, I have not been sitting around, so please enjoy a couple of select projects that I've been apart of - or I've made myself - below.
Thank you for visiting my little corner of the web, have fun!

Music / Soundtrack
